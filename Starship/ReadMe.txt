Starship Foundation (SD1-A01)

Build Instructions:
1. Open Starship.sln in Visual Studio.
2. Build the solution in Debug or Release mode for x64.
3. The executable will be placed in SD/Starship/Run as Starship_[Debug|Release]_x64.exe.

How to Play:
- Three triangular ships will spawn at the left edge and travel east at different speeds.
- The game ends when any ship reaches the right edge of the screen.

Controls:
- T: Hold to slow time to 1/10th speed.
- P: Pause/unpause the game.
- O: Step forward one frame, then pause.
- Q: Quit the game.

Known Issues: None
